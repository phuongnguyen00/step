Activities

At Pomona College, I work at several on-campus offices and organizations.

I have been a Tour Guide for the Admissions Office since the second semester of my first year. Leading tours is always a lot of fun because I get to know many amazing high school students and could offer a little help with their college application journeys. I also participated in other exciting activities organized by the office, which includes the shooting of Sagehen Next, a parody of Ariana Grande's "Thank you, Next". This music video was produced as a part of a series of activities to welcome Pomona Class of 2023.

I also work at Office of Housing and Residence Life as a Residence Advisor. I enjoy organizing events for my residents and collaborating with on-campus offices, organizations and other RAs. Two social events that are most memorable to me are the Note-taking 101 workshop, where we learned about note-taking tips and brush lettering techniques, and the Ice Cream Social, where we chatted over icecream as we got to know each other in the beginning of the school year.

I'm also employed at the Career Development Office as a Student Assistant, where I perform front desk duties and help students navigate the resources available at the office. We are also proud to be one of the best places for coffee in Claremont, according to Voices, Pomona College Student Blog.

In my free time, I enjoy doing brush lettering, reading, and practicing yoga.
